Dan Laurie

Instrumentalist, Producer

I have been playing bass since I was 10 years old and since then it's given me some amazing opportunities from playing at Ronnie Scotts to collaborating with artists such as Rick Wakeman, Dave Holland and Dave Liebmann.

I'm a classically trained Double Bass player, but also an experienced electric bass player covering genres from Jazz to Progressive Rock.

Playing music has also led me down the path of audio editing and sound design. I've produced and mixed my own tracks and for local artists such as Cian Ducrot and Harry and The Chicks.

I'm a musican, have worked with various different musicians and now I'm looking to move into management and marketing within the music industry.
